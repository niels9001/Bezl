# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** April 14, 2026

## Overview

Bezl is a desktop screenshot tool that runs entirely on your device. Your privacy is important — Bezl does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

## Data Collection

Bezl does **not** collect any of the following:

- Personal information
- Usage or analytics data
- Screenshots or images
- Telemetry or crash reports
- Network requests to external servers

## Local Storage

Bezl stores the following data locally on your device:

- **Settings** (default padding, corner radius) in `~/.bezl/settings.json`
- **Custom gradients** in `~/.bezl/gradients.json`
- **Wallpaper images** in `~/Pictures/Bezl/`

This data never leaves your device and can be deleted at any time by removing the `.bezl` folder from your user profile directory.

## Third-Party Services

Bezl does not integrate with or send data to any third-party services.

## Changes

If this privacy policy changes, the updated version will be posted in this repository.
